TwitterBot with JavaScript

Milk and Gech is a TwitterBot that tweets posts from the Overheard at GT Facebook Group in a form that resembles poetry. The idea for this bot came from the book Milk and Vines, which is a parody of Rupi Kaur's Milk and Honey. For an additional humorous effect, this bot tweets memes with nouns replaced with Georgia Tech-related words.

— Milk & Gech (@milkandgech) October 31, 2018

Java/Processing Projects

Redraw The Stars:

A drawing tool that allows users to place stars in the night sky and create their own constellations. This program utlizes an audio library so the connecting lines and background stars change color depending on the volume in the room.

Laser Beam Builder Game:

A game consisting of multiple players in which one player arranges obstacles with mirrors and blocks for the second player to figure out how to reach the target with one or more lasers. Working with a group and taking on the role of Craft Engineer for this project, I contributed to designing the functionality of the game and to the UI design of the game menu. Travel Troopers App & Map Prototype

While studying abroad in Georgia Tech's Barcelona Summer Program (2018), I took a class on User Interface Design. For our group project throughout the entire course, my team and I created a prototype of an app and augmented reality map interface that helped groups traveling abroad find the best local restaurants as recommended by their friends. In addition to recommendations, our app and map interfaces included the ability to provide directions to these locations and facilitate communication among groups of friends.

Hi! My name is Fernanda and my pronouns are she/her/hers. My degree in Computational Media has allowed me to develop my skills as a software engineer, experience designer, and digital storyteller. In any technology I design or build, my top priority is putting people first and enhancing the user experience. With my concentration in Film & Media, I've enhanced my abilities in communicating stories through videos as well as through writing and conversation. I consider myself a storyteller because I enjoy writing articles on Medium for fun, filming and editing videos, and mentoring any new or prospective Georgia Tech students.
